Biography

Short Bio

    Wenshuai Zhao is currently a Phd. student at the Department of Electronic Engineering and Automation, Aalto University, Finland. Before that, in 2020 he obtained his master degree from Fudan University and University of Turku. He graduated from Central South University with the bachelor degree in 2014.

Education and Experience

  • Sep. 2017 - Jan. 2020: M.E., Electronics and Communication Engineering, Fudan University, China.
    Supervisor: Yuanyuan Wang
    Thesis topic: VHL gene mutation prediction of ccRCC based on CT images. Poster.

  • Jan. 2018 - Jun. 2018: M.S., Double Degree in Information and Communication Technology, University of Turku, Finland.
    Supervisor: Juha Plosila

  • Jul. 2014 - May. 2017: Electrical Engineer, China General Nuclear Power Group, Shenzhen, China.

  • Sep. 2010 - Jun. 2014: B.E., Mechanical Design, Manufacture & Automation, Central South University, China.
    Supervisor: Jianping Tan
    Thesis topic: Level control system for manufacturing aluminum sheet.
